Gringo Starr

Peace, love, and foosball
Some call him a hippie. We call him Gringo. He is a free spirit and is full of love. While being least competitive member of the SFL, Gringo brings one of top defenses in the league and his offensive is primarly scraps, put-backs, and rebounds. His 3 bar is sneaky, especially after 12 beers.

He lives off the land and truly believes that everyone has something positive to contribute to the world. Gringo loves everyone and always brings words of encouragement, even during the most tense times. Don't be surprised if he calls a timeout just to say that he loves you. We love you too Gringo, chingos.
