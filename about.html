About The SFL

What

What is the SFL? Think of your favorite wrestling company: Bad ass characters, cool promos, trash talk, champions, tag team matches, slamming fans beers. SFL aims to be that...but substitute the wrestling for foosball.

Why

The SFL is a league where partying and chingasos are as important to us as foosball is.

We love foosball but we are not fond of foosball leagues. Things are way too serious and the rule books are a bit excessive. Examples:
  • ITSF: 22.2 Cursing by a player shall not be allowed.
  • SFL Response: What kind of dumb shit is this?
  • ITSF: 20.2 Talking between teammates while the ball is in play may be judged a distraction.
  • SFL: Talking between teammates is encouraged. Also, talking shit to the other team mid-play is HIGHLY encouraged.
  • ITSF: 20.5 A player who removes his hands from the handles and reaches down or away from the table (to wipe his hands, apply rosin, etc) while the ball is in play shall be charged with a distraction.
  • SFL: Well first off, women play too. Secondly, what if you're thirsty mid-play for some cold beer? Fuck this rule.
